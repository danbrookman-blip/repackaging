The Value Exchange

Give guests a reason to share. Earn the next visit.

It's the idea Airship is built around. Offer your guests something in return for their data, then keep giving on every visit. The more they engage, the more you know, and the more often they come back and spend.

1

Bring all your data together

Every booking, EPOS sale, Wi-Fi login, review and sign-up flows into one place. Nothing left on the table.

2

Understand who the guest is

One enriched profile per guest: who they are, what they like and how often they actually visit.

3

Give something back

Reward the sign-up, the birthday and every visit with offers worth sharing their data for. That's the exchange.

4

They come back, more often

Visit frequency and spend climb, and every visit feeds fresh data straight back into the loop.

The loop compounds: richer data sharpens every offer, which earns the next visit, which feeds richer data.
