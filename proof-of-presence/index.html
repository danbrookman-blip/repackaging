How it works

Three steps from signal to revenue

Connect the signals

Wi-Fi logins, bookings, EPOS spend and reward redemptions flow into Airship as visit signals.

Verify the visit

Airship matches those signals to a guest profile and confirms a genuine, in-venue presence.
