Pricing questions, answered

How is the platform priced?

Per location per month, on a rate card that scales down as your estate grows, plus active email contacts above your included allowance. Each package includes an allowance per site (10k Capture, 50k Grow, 100k Scale).

Can I upgrade as I grow?

Yes. The three packages map to where you are: Capture to bring guests back, Grow to scale across sites, Scale to run marketing at estate level. Move up whenever the job changes.

Are modules included?

No. Modules like Airship Lookout and Cheers by Airship are separate products with their own buyer and budget, bought alongside your package rather than bundled into a tier.
