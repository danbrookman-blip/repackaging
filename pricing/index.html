Pricing questions, answered

What is an Active Email Contact?

An opted-in contact in one of the top three engagement states (New, Highly engaged or Engaged). Contacts that lapse to unengaged or dormant stop billing, so you only pay for an audience that's actually reading.

How is the platform priced?

Per location per month, on a rate card that scales down as your estate grows, plus active email contacts above your included allowance. Each package includes an allowance per site (10k Connect, 50k Grow, 100k Orchestrate).

Can I upgrade as I grow?

Yes. The three packages map to where you are: Connect to bring guests back, Grow to scale across sites, Orchestrate to run marketing at estate level. Move up whenever the job changes.

Are modules included?

No. Modules like Airship Lookout and Cheers by Airship are separate products with their own buyer and budget, bought alongside your package rather than bundled into a tier.
