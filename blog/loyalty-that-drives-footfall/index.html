Designing a loyalty scheme that actually drives footfall

9 April 2026 · 7 min · Airship Team

Most loyalty schemes reward people who'd have come anyway. Here's how to build one that changes behaviour.

A loyalty scheme is only worth running if it changes behaviour: bringing guests back sooner, more often, or spending more when they do.

The trick is to reward verified visits, not just sign-ups. With Proof of Presence®, every reward redemption is a real visit you can measure.
