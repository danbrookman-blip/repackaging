Make your data work

The data is already yours

Who, where, when, how much. Every meaningful interaction a guest has with your venue is a Proof of Presence, a signal you can act on. We've recorded 81 million of them. The brands that win are the ones that engage with that data, not the ones that collect the most.

Where it comes from

Two ways the data arrives

Active collection

The guest opts in. A newsletter sign-up, a form, a single deliberate action to join your CRM.

Passive collection

The majority of it. Captured automatically through your integrations and transactions, every time a guest visits, books or spends.

Both are rich. Most brands simply leave the passive half sitting there.

Where guests are

Every guest sits in one of four states

Dormant

No visits or email activity in the last 18 months.

Occasional

A single visit in the last 18 months.

Active

More than one visit in the last 18 months.

Lapsed

Active or Occasional guests who have slipped back to Dormant.

Guests don't stay still. They move between these states all year. The only question is which direction you move them.

Turning data into revenue

Five loyal guests are worth thirty-eight casual ones

Score every guest on two things

GES and GVS

Your most loyal guests are the ones who engage more with your marketing and spend more. Plot the two scores together and four clear segments appear.

How often and how recently they engage.

Play to each segment

How to treat each segment

Casual

£25 / guest

Visitors with low email engagement.

Lean on recency. Time-sensitive offers with a short expiry to drive urgency: “we'd love to see you again, here's 10% off your next visit.”

Follower

£36 / guest

High email interaction but low visits.

Loves your emails, light on visits. Focus on brand story, seasonal moments, booking in advance and your social channels.

Regular

£131 / guest

Moderate visit frequency and engagement.

Build frequency with a reliable reason to return: think Wine Wednesdays, a standing offer or a tasting.

Loyal

£200 / guest

High visit frequency, high spend, strong email engagement.

Surprise and delight. A personalised, in-venue reward is what keeps your most valuable guests coming back.

Deliver a clear Value Exchange

Win-win moments that build a relationship

The Value Exchange is about win-win moments that build a long-term relationship. Four moves:

  • Take all your data through the same funnel.
  • Deliver the promise you made when guests signed up, through automated journeys.
  • Focus on your real revenue opportunities.
  • Make sure guests see clear value in every single interaction.
